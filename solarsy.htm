The Solar System

  • Sun

    Distance from Earth: About 93 million miles (150 million kilometers)
    Diameter: Approximately 1.39 million kilometers
    Mass: About 330,000 times that of Earth
    Gravity: 27.9 times that of Earth
    Composition: Mostly hydrogen and helium
    Temperature: Core temperature reaches up to 27 million°F (15 million°C)
    Surface Temperature: Around 10,000°F (5,500°C)
    Age: Estimated to be around 4.6 billion years

  • Mercury

    Closest to the Sun.
    Smallest planet in our solar system.
    Average distance from the Sun: 57.9 million kilometers.
    No moons.
    Thin atmosphere composed mostly of oxygen, sodium, and hydrogen.
    Weak gravitational pull.

  • Venus

    Second planet from the Sun.
    Similar in size and structure to Earth.
    Average distance from the Sun: 108.2 million kilometers.
    No moons.
    Thick, toxic atmosphere with clouds of sulfuric acid.
    Surface temperature can exceed 460°C due to a strong greenhouse effect.

  • Earth

    Third planet from the Sun.
    Only astronomical object known to support life.
    Average distance from the Sun: 149.6 million kilometers.
    One moon.
    Atmosphere composed mainly of nitrogen and oxygen.
    Supports vast water bodies, including oceans.

  • Mars

    Distance from the Sun: 142 million miles (228 million kilometers)
    Diameter: 4,220 miles (6,779 kilometers)
    Mass: 0.107 times that of Earth
    Gravity: 0.377 times that of Earth
    Atmosphere: Thin, composed mostly of carbon dioxide
    Temperature: -195°F to 70°F -125°C to 20°C

  • Jupiter

    Distance from the Sun: 484 million miles (778 million kilometers)
    Diameter: 86,881 miles (139,822 kilometers)
    Mass: 318 times that of Earth
    Gravity: 2.528 times that of Earth
    Atmosphere: Composed mostly of hydrogen and helium
    Temperature: -234°F (-145°C)

  • Saturn

    Distance from the Sun: 888 million miles (1.4 billion kilometers)
    Diameter: 72,366 miles (116,460 kilometers)
    Mass: 95 times that of Earth
    Gravity: 1.065 times that of Earth
    Atmosphere: Composed mostly of hydrogen and helium
    Temperature: -288°F (-178°C)

  • Uranus

    Distance from the Sun: 1.8 billion miles (2.9 billion kilometers)
    Diameter: 31,518 miles (50,724 kilometers)
    Mass: 14.5 times that of Earth
    Gravity: 0.886 times that of Earth
    Atmosphere: Composed mostly of hydrogen, helium, and methane
    Temperature: -357°F (-214°C)

  • Neptune

    Distance from the Sun: 2.8 billion miles (4.3 billion kilometers)
    Diameter: 30,598 miles (49,244 kilometers)
    Mass: 17 times that of Earth
    Gravity: 1.137 times that of Earth
    Atmosphere: Composed mostly of hydrogen, helium, and methane
    Temperature: -353°F (-214°C)
